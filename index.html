Fried Rice Tutorial

About This Project

What we Made: This tutorial teaches viewers how to cook delicious fried rice with vegetables, using simple steps and engaging multimedia content.

Why We Made It: Fried rice is a universal dish that’s both easy to make and enjoyable. We wanted to help learners develop cooking skills while having fun in the kitchen.

How We Made It: We include a video tutorial, designed an infographic, and included a feedback form for users to share their creations. This project applies multimedia principles like cognitive load theory to make learning easy and enjoyable.

Fried Rice Tutorial

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Prepare your ingredients: chop vegetables, cook the rice if not already done.
  2. Heat a pan and scramble the eggs. Set aside.
  3. Stir-fry the vegetables with garlic until tender.
  4. Add the rice and soy sauce, mixing everything well.
  5. Stir in the scrambled eggs. Cook for another 2 minutes and serve!
